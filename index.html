Who We Are

A legacy of trust, bio-security, and high-quality livestock breeding in Maharashtra.

Anand Sagar Poultry Farm is a modern poultry breeding facility nestled in Khamkhed, Jalgaon Jamod. We take pride in raising healthy, top-grade boiler chickens in state-of-the-art facilities designed for maximum bio-security and stress-free bird management.

By blending advanced automation systems with precise nutrition profiles, we ensure every bird leaving our farm meets the highest standards of safety, quality, and weight consistency.
